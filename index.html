About Me

I am a Computer Engineering graduate student at Razi University, specializing in Computer Architecture. My research focuses on Intrusion Detection in heterogeneous Internet of Things using distributed learning methods. I have extensive experience in Federated Learning, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, and Data Analytics.

With a background in both Computer Engineering and Petroleum Engineering, I bring a unique perspective to my work. I've been working with multiple technologies and have experience in various projects related to AI, image processing, and data analysis.

Currently, I'm ranked 1st among the Master of Science students in Computer Engineering at Razi University, and I'm actively involved in research at the High Performance Computing Laboratory (HPC Lab).
